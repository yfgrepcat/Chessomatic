## Graphical Analysis of how our model performs against Stockfish at different levels

### Important Note

This folder contains the analysis results of our different models.
**The main results of our research are to be found under the stockfish10_basic folder.**

The stockfish10_neural folder contains the results of the neural linucb model, which we trained for a shorter amount of time and with a somewhat perfectible neural network. Thus, they are only to be considered as a proof of concept, and not as a real competitor to the basic linucb model.
